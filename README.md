# Truth of Abyss (For now)

###  Author: Yuchen Jiang

### Game type

Simple text-based RPG game

### Description

An unknown abyss has emerged near you hometown.
You are a rookie adventurer, and you decide to take your first steps into the unknown.

At the beginning of your journey, you will be granted a set of random talents.
Some talents may aid you greatly in your adventure — while others may become a burden you must overcome.

During your exploration, you will face countless events, gain experience, meet companions who travel with you, and confront enemies who seek to stop you.

Can you survive the trials ahead and uncover the truth behind the mysterious abyss?
